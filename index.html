    Recycling is the top action society can do to simultaneously improve: the environment, the economy, sustainable manufacturing and to prevent waste from going into oceans.

    Our Mission

    We are committed to helping improve the environment through reducing Lee High School's renvironmental impact and cost. Our goal is to reduce the amount of waste produced by the school through various recycling measures. Find more about what we intend to do on the Action Plan and Budget pages.

    Why Recycle?

    Waste has a huge negative impact on the environment, like contributing to global warming and harming wild animals and the natural environment. Recyling helps to reduce this impact. Recycling initivatives have been taken up by schools all over the state, and we want Lee High School to join them in working to heal the environment. Find out more about recycling on the Action Plan page.
